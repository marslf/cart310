# Interview Questions

**1. Friendship Values & Meaning**

What qualities or behaviors do you value most in a friendship?

Can you describe a friendship that felt especially meaningful to you? What made it that way?

How do you usually show care or support to your friends? How do you like to receive it?

**2. Interaction & Maintenance**

How do you usually stay in touch with your friends? What works best for you?

Do you think it’s important to actively maintain friendships? 

Have you ever unintentionally lost touch with a friend? What contributed to that?

**3. Reflection & Tools**

What would make it easier for you to maintain friendships over time?

If an app could help you track or strengthen friendships, what would be most useful to see or do?
